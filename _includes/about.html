About Me

My path to becoming a Designer/Developer began in a high school Computer Science course. It was there that I discovered the amazing things one could create with a few keystrokes & a little know-how. While my first project (a reimagination of the classic Pong) was far from what I would consider even passible design & coding work, it sparked my interest in what I love to do now. Creating engaging & attractive things with computers.
